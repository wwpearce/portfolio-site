The UX:

Users of Destination: Dunkirk were given three episodic, choose-your-own-adventure-style audio games. This Alexa Skill told the story leading up to the film Dunkirk, giving user the option of playing as a foot soldier, tank-commander, or pilot. Users chose from a list of possible actions to navigate the story tree.

My Part:

I was part of a three person team who ideated, wrote outlines and scripts, built prototypes, pitched, evaluated voice-over, sounds, illustrations, and oversaw technical production.
